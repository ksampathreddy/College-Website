Transport

Our transport facility connects the corners of the twin cities (Hyderabad and secunderabad) to our college DRKCET have a fleet of 15 buses for the Transport of the Students and Staff members . Buses reach the college by 9:00AM and leave the college in the evening by 4:30PM.

A Nominal fee is collected towards the Bus fare from the Students on the Basis of their boarding point. Transport is FREE for all the staff members. The details are available with the transport incharge All the buses are maintained well with regular servicing and maintainence.
