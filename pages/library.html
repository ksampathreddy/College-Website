Library

DRKIST has a library with over 26807 volumes of Text/ Reference/ Hand Books. Library subscribes more than 68 National Journals and have also large number of e-resources in the form of DVDs and CDs apart from Reports/ Theses/ Dissertations. The Library operations are fully automated. Library also provides suitable reprographic facilities to students and teachers. A well-equipped Library is an icon of quality education in its supreme form. As a New Age library, it provides unlimited access to information in many formats and from many sources. To students at DRKIST, it offers a perfect platform for research, innovation and development and a tranquil ambience to explore the world of books. To begin with, the Central Library is in sync with the latest in technology, offering a balanced combination of print and digital collections. The online databases like IEEE, ASME, ASCE, J-Gate Ecooshnet, Knimbus and membership of digital consortia DELNET and other professional bodies have opened up a wealth of opportunities for scholars and students alike.

Digital Library and a separate enclosure for Research and Reference, the Central Library is slowly but surely inculcating a reading habit among the young and restless.
