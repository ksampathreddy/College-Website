Objectives of the club

Academic and extra-curricular activities, to encourage students to actively participate in related events. The club mission is to bring out the hidden talents among students and also provide them a stage where they can showcase their arts and cultural talent and interest.

The club provides lot of opportunities in the form of competitions and workshops for igniting the spark of interest present in the budding technocrats and honing their skills towards showcasing their talents. It provides a platform to students to showcase their talent in music, dance other fine arts and sports and Encourages managerial capabilities such as event management and stage organization among students and to encourage students to actively participate in related events.

The main objectives are

  • Attract the broadest possible audience and new audience.
  • Increase capacity and knowledge of event planning, event management and even tourism.
  • Increase social cohesion of the locality.
  • Stimulate and promote the creative economy of the location.
