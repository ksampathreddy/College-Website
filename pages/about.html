About Us

The era of success of DRK Institute of Science & Technology has started way back in 2004 when it was established by Mr. D.B Chandra Sekhar Rao in the fond memory of his late father Sri Dasari Rama Kotaiah garu. Mr. D.B Chandra Shekar Rao is an eminent & successful entrepreneur, who has subsequently decided to focus on high quality education with the philanthropic motive, to act as a leader to promote economic & industrial growth of the country.

DRK Institute of Science & Technology has envisaged significant investment of capital and resources to give shape to the development of the institute, in terms of Infrastructure, academic resources and career development for its students. A vibrant effort has been made to consciously invest to improve the conceptual and technical skill of the students.

DRK Institutes of Science & Technology features excellent Infrastructure, modern engineering labs with the latest equipment, experienced faculties and lots of opportunities for development through our collaborations with our other organizations of the DRK Group and close ties with the best companies of the IT Sector.

Vision

To be a Premier Institution with sky-scraping educational values and research built on communal trust and instill high pattern of discipline to serve the nation and the world.

Mission

To implant in students, the aptitude values and vision that will prepare them to lead lives of personal integrity and civic responsibility.

Quality

To Provide Quality Education to the Students to excel in the fields of Engineering & Management to cater to the ever Challenging needs.
