Students

For the all-round development of the students and to enrich their technical knowledge the DRK group of institutions provides a platform like clubs for various student activities.

Objectives of the clubs:

  • To be a source of motivation
  • To promote academic excellence by offering academic support
  • To make members aware of technological advancements at present.
  • To enhance the knowledge of students in System Dynamics and to explain the concepts in a practical way and help them to gain knowledge.
  • Attract the broadest possible audience and new audience.
  • Increase capacity and knowledge of event planning, event management and even tourism.
  • Increase social cohesion of the locality.
  • Stimulate and promote the creative economy of the location.
  • Bringing out the best in its members by conducting events that enhance personality traits
  • To help people, make such use of their own capacities and of environmental resources as well which result in personal satisfaction and adjustment
  • Change the environment in favor of individuals growth & development
