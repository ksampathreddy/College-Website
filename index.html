DRK Institute of Science & Technology has envisaged significant investment of capital and resources to give clients and colleagues out of the mediums, in terms of infrastructure, academic resources and caring development for its students. A vibrant effort has been made to consciously invest in improve the conceptual and technical skills of the students.
